FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

For Sellers

  1. How do I register as a seller?

    If you're new and interested in becoming a seller on [Your Marketplace Name], you can sign up by visiting the "Sign Up" page. There, you'll find a Google Form that you need to fill out with your details. Once submitted, our team will review your application, and if approved, we'll contact you to proceed with the registration process.

  2. How do I log in as a seller?

    If you're a registered seller, you can log in by clicking on the "Login as Seller" button. Enter the email and password provided by the admin, and you'll gain access to your seller dashboard.

  3. Is there a fee for using Otlob?

    No, Otlob is free to use for both buyers and sellers. There are no hidden fees or subscription charges. You can browse, buy, and sell items without any additional costs.

For Customers

  1. How do I browse products on Otlob?

    To browse products on Otlob, you can either scroll through the homepage or use the search bar located at the top of the page. Simply enter keywords related to the product you're looking for, and relevant results will be displayed.

  2. How can I contact a seller?

    Contacting a seller on Otlob is easy. Once you find an item you like, click on it to view the product details. From there, you'll find a button that says "Contact Seller." Click on this button, and you'll be redirected to WhatsApp, where you can directly message the seller to inquire about the product or place an order.

  3. How do I create an account?

    Creating an account on Otlob is simple. Click on the "Sign Up" button located at the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in the required information, and your account will be created instantly.

  4. Do I need to sign in to browse products?

    No, you don't need to sign in to browse products on Otlob. Feel free to explore our platform and discover a wide range of items without having to create an account.

  5. What if I have questions about a product?

    If you have any questions about a product listed on Otlob, you can reach out to the seller directly using the "Contact Seller" button on the product page. The seller will be happy to provide you with more information and assist you with your purchase.
